## Plan

Here you can integrate the modules into the calendar by dragging and dropping. You can open the drag and drop menu by clicking on the three dots at the top right.

![image-20220211213613512](Plan.assets/image-20220211213613512.png)

## Module overview

You can switch between the plan and the module overview using the arrow at the top left of "Plan". The overview shows you in which month which module is due. 

![image-20220211213628403](Plan.assets/image-20220211213628403.png)
